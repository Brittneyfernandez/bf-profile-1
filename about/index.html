So who am I?

I’m Brittney Fernandez. I am 32 years old. I was born in Texas and raised In Fresno California I am a mother of 3 children. I began my journey in graphic design by attending Bitwise Industries online course for coding while doing so. I was later offered an apprenticeship for Alphaworks in graphic design. I was able to learn and execute the elements and creativity of design. I started working with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Indesign, and Figma. I gained tons of feedback and helpful tools by working with professional designers on real-life projects for Bitwise Industries. I gained a passion for design and being creative while also working with my team. I’m eager to continue expanding my knowledge, creativity and working on more projects for my future clients.
